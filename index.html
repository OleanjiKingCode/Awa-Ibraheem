AWA Ibraheem's WORLD

DR AWA is a Chatered Accountant with a PhD in Management Science (Ilorin), an MBA (Zaria), and a first-class degree in Accounting (Lagos). He has over 40 years of experience in accounting, auditing, tax management, financial management, stock broking and revenue generation consulting. He was a Hubert Humphrey fellow at University of Texas at Austin, USA in 1992/93 academic year. He had attended courses in many reputable institutions all over the world including Strathclyde University, Glasgow, International Labour Organisation Trainning Center, Turin, Italy and Havard University, USA. He was commissioned as an Honorary Texan . . .by the State Of Texas, USA, in July, 1993. He was also a 1992 University of Lagos Distinguished Alumni Award winner and 2019 Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria Merit Award winner. He is currently the Pro Chancellor of Fountain University, Osogbo and the President of Ikeja Golf Club in Lagos State. He is associated with a number of organisations active in the private sector some of which are on this page.
